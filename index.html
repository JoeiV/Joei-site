About Me

Hi! I'm Jo Leonard. My journey has taken many twists and turns, though always fueled by the desire to make the world better in some way. The current leg of my journey led me to Washington, DC, where I learned many fun and efficient ways to mine and manage data of all kinds. I recently completed my Masters in Computer Science, specializing in - you guessed it - Data Management!

In addition to my love of data, I have been coding websites and apps in mostly JavaScript - but also using Ruby, Python, and PHP - since 2015.

When I am not coding or diving into data, I am usually running or enjoying live music (*looking forward to returning to live music when we get this pandemic under control).
